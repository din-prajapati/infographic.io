## Story

*As a* real estate agent
*I want* the suggestion chips below the AI's reply to be relevant to my current conversation
*So that* I can quickly pick the right next step without typing

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC1:** After every AI reply, exactly 3 suggestion chips appear below the message — generated by GPT-4o as part of the same `ConversationAiService.chat()` call (already returns `chips: string[]`)
- [ ] **AC2:** Chips are contextually relevant: during data gathering they suggest providing more info; post-generation they suggest refinements or variations
- [ ] **AC3:** Chips do not contain model names, resolution numbers, or technical jargon — they use real estate language ("Add open house details", "Make it more luxurious", "Create a Just Sold version")
- [ ] **AC4:** Clicking a chip submits its text as the next user message (existing behavior preserved)
- [ ] **AC5:** The static `promptSuggestionsData.ts` is no longer the source for chips shown after an AI response (may still be used for the empty-state initial prompt list)
- [ ] **AC6:** `npm run check` passes

---

## Out of Scope

- Editing or dismissing individual chips
- Persisting chip selections to DB
- More than 3 chips per response
- Initial empty-state chips (keep static list for that)
