## Story

As a **listing agent**, I want **Just Listed → Open House → Price Improved → Just Sold assets generated from the listing I already entered**, so that **each milestone of the sale gets marketing without re-entering anything**.

## Acceptance Criteria (draft)

- [ ] **AC1** — Listing (kit) has a lifecycle stage; changing it offers stage-specific asset generation (banner text, badge, urgency styling per stage)
- [ ] **AC2** — Stage assets reuse the original extraction + brand profile; only stage-dependent text/badges change
- [ ] **AC3** — Open House stage accepts date/time; rendered exactly (verify layer)
- [ ] **AC4** — Just Sold auto-suggests a "sold" variant of the hero asset
